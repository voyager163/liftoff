# Code of Conduct

Liftoff contributors and maintainers are expected to make participation respectful, constructive, and welcoming.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss ideas and technical decisions in good faith.
- Give actionable feedback and assume reasonable intent.
- Respect differing backgrounds, identities, experience levels, and viewpoints.
- Keep public communication focused on the project and its community.
- Protect private or sensitive information shared during security and conduct reports.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, personal attacks, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation, repeated disruption, and publication of another person's private information are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove content, close interactions, restrict participation, or ban contributors when necessary to protect the community.

Report urgent or sensitive conduct concerns privately through the repository's vulnerability reporting form and identify the report as a conduct concern. Reports will be reviewed as confidentially as practical, with action based on context, severity, and repeated behavior.
